How to Use These Training Pages

  1. Display on Computer: Open one of the training pages below on your desktop or laptop screen
  2. Open Verific App: On your phone, go to paul-hammant.github.io/verific
  3. Start Camera: Click "Start Camera" in the app
  4. Align Registration Marks: Position your phone so the black square registration marks in the app align with the squares on the training page
  5. Capture & Verify: Click "Capture & Verify" to scan and verify the certification
  6. Check Results: The app will extract text, compute the hash, and verify against the certification database
